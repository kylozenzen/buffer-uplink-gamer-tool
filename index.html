KEY
Credential safety

Your Buffer key is basically a stream key for your social accounts.

It can authorize publishing. That makes it powerful, useful, and absolutely not something to flash on-screen during a “just chatting” stream.

What Uplink does with it

Keep control of the credential.

Uplink stores the token in this browser’s local storage. Requests pass through the included stateless Netlify function to Buffer; Uplink does not require its own user account or database.

Do not share itNever send it to collaborators, paste it into chat, or include it in a screenshot.
Use a trusted deploymentDeploy your own copy or inspect the included source before connecting.
Rotate after exposureIf the key appears on stream or in public code, revoke it and create a fresh one.
